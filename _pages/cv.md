## Profile

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Paul Scherrer Institut (PSI), working on high-pressure hydrothermal reaction systems for sustainable energy and environmental applications. My work combines experimental analysis, reactor-level thinking, and computational methods to understand hydrogen-rich gas production, catalytic performance, salt behavior, and transport phenomena in complex reacting systems.

## Research Interests

- Supercritical water gasification and hydrothermal waste treatment
- Hydrogen production and reactor design optimization
- Catalytic hydrothermal reactions and catalyst stability
- Salt crystallization, deposition, and desalination in supercritical water
- Heat and mass transfer in multiphase flows and thermochemical reactions
- Molecular dynamics simulations, computational fluid dynamics, and mechanism analysis

## Technical Expertise

**Computational and modelling tools**

- Computational fluid dynamics: Ansys Fluent, SpaceClaim, Tecplot 360, CFD-Post
- Molecular dynamics: Materials Studio, LAMMPS, OVITO
- Process and engineering tools: Aspen Plus, AutoCAD, Origin, Visio, ImageJ
- Research workflow: EndNote, scientific visualization, data analysis, AI-assisted literature and writing workflows

**Experimental and analytical methods**

- Thermal and gas analysis: Elemental analysis, TOC/TN
- Composition and trace analysis: Ion chromatography, HPLC
- High-pressure hydrothermal reaction and product analysis workflows
